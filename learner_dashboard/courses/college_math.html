Introduction about the course series

Hi! I'm Wenny Wei, the founder of Mathera. I have designed and organized all content in Mathera, including the mind-maps, insights, and problem-solving. When I was self studying such amazing college courses in MIT OCW back in 9th grade, I was deeply inspired and grateful for the availability for such high quality, free educational recourse. At the same time, I wished there would be a platform that inspirates young learners, and guides them through the learning journey together. That's why I reorganized, collected, and polished all my lecture notes for MIT OCW lectures, then transformed them into a comprehensive study guide and creative course structures that YOU will have a better view about math. Later, I founded Mathera as a system that teaches you how to think, connect, and create in mathematics. Let's model the math world together!

To gain the most and use this platform effectively, I hightly encourage you to view the online video lectures in MIT OCW first, then use the mind-maps, self quiz, and other learning material as a reinforcement and an interpretation aid. I believe you will definitely find our course series useful and inspirational!

Lastly, I convey my immense graditude for MIT OpenCourseWare!
