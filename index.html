Why is thinking essential

Many people see math as formulas, problems, or exams. But what is math, truly? Or, let me ask you this question: if you are a good problem solver, can you model math? Do you have a picture of math? You see the problem: what you mostly learnt is linear. If formulas and problems are the ingredients, then what you are about to discover here is the recipe.

Because in the end, what truly distinguishes one learner from another is not how many problems they can solve, but how they think.

Knowledge is always accessible. Thinking is what makes you unique.

At Mathera, our goal is simple: to help you build a thinking system that turns you from a learner into a creator.

The thinking system

Now you may wonder what is this thinking system exactly? To answer that, I will introduce my own thinking strategy -- Mindmapping learning. This is definitely not a simple topic-collector, this is your own logic circuit. Here is an example:

Think of it like this: you first enter a forest, all you know is the location of the exit, only your mapping system tells you how to get there.

Instead of seeing math as isolated topics, we guide you to:

- connect ideas

- recognize patterns

- understand why concepts are introduced

- explore what remains unknown

See? When others are gathering ingredients, you are building the assembly line.

This study strategy may sound overcomplexing or even overwhelming, but it will indeed boost your learning efficiency! That's exactly what you will gain – ‘easy studying method’ such as reading a textbook usually corresponds to a low percentage of gain, while something that first feels challenging is what helps you improve. Over time, you will find it natural to think following your own pattern, you know how to model math and become a creative explorer!
