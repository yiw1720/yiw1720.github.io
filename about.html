About Me

Me as a highschooler

Hi! My name is Wenny Wei. I am currently a 9th grader who has strong interest in math and CS. Whether you are in middle schools, high schools, or colleges, have you ever felt the teacher/professor is describing an amazying thing in class, and you seem to understand it; homework and exams are not a big task for you. However, when you ask yourself a deeper question: Why is this always true? Is it an obvious phenomenon? Can I do it in a smarter way? You think hard but lose your direction, you have curiosity, you know you are confused, but just can't GET IT? Well, you are trying to BUILD THE PICTURE OF MATH, not simply FINISH THE PROBLEM. LEARNER--THINKER--CREATOR These are the three stages of studying anything, not just math. Ultimately, you are becoming a creator; therefore, you need to first THINK. You need to construct a THINKING SYSTEM that applies to your daily lives. Good news? This is the right place!

What this website will provide

Either you are still struggling with basic concepts, or you want to seek advanced math studying material, this website will provide a comprehensive study guide that help you think CRITICALLY and LOGICALLY. Knowledge is NOT LINEAR, studying various topics or techniques piecewisely makes the 'mission' tedious. From my notes, you will learn how to connect topics, how to analysize a problem, and how to apply your creative thoughts. You will also be able to establish your own learning path as well!

How to use this website

If you are good at independent studying, I highly recommend you to challenge yourself by first studying the topics with the materials I provided, then use my mind map (Active-recall version) for enhancing. If you feel lost, don't worry! My notes also aim to guide you through those challenges while having fun!
